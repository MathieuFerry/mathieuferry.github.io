Mathieu Ferry

I am a sociologist interested in social stratification and the articulation between ascriptive position—such as caste, religion, ethnicity—and class resources—including educational credentials.

My projects use both quantitative and qualitative methods to refine our understanding of social stratification processes by examining societies—such as India—or social groups—such as immigrants and their descendants in France—that are generally less often the subject of studies analyzing lifestyles, status processes and social mobility.

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Ined and I take part in the 3GEN project.
